Crews

U.S. Forest Service handcrews, which typically consist of 20 men and women, serve as the infantry of wildland firefighters. There are five types of handcrews: Type 1 Interagency Hotshot Crews (IHC), Type 1, Type 2 – Initial Attack (IA),and Type 2.

Working side by side, the crews’ main responsibilities are to construct “firelines” – which look a lot like trails – around wildfires to contain and control them by removing fuel that wildfires need to spread. To do this, handcrews use tools like Pulaski's, shovels, and chainsaws to clear flammable materials and scrape or dig to mineral soil. Hand crews also burn-out fire areas, and mop up after fires.

Hotshots

Hotshots (IHC) are the most highly skilled type of handcrews and are typically assigned to work on the most challenging parts of wildfires. Within the limits of their experience and qualifications, IHCs are capable of providing a disciplined, self –contained and adaptable workforce to meet the needs of incident managers in a variety of situations and during all hazard assignments.

Type 2IA

Type 2 IA crews are the second most skilled type of handcrew and are typically assigned to work in smaller modules of 5-6 personel. Within the qualified modules they can respond to multiple incidents of small fires. Typically this will occur after a lightning storm has multiple strikes to the ground.

Fireline Capability Initial attack/ can be broken up into squads
Fireline construction
Firing to include burnout
Crew Size 18-20
Leadership Qualifications Crew Boss: CRWB
3 Squad Bosses: ICT5
Experience 60% 1 season

Type 2

Type 2 crews are the lowest skilled type of handcrew and are typically assigned to work on quiet areas of large fires and do not split up into smaller modules. Within the qualified modules they can respond to new wildfires and construct firelines. Most type 2 crews are pulled together when needed from various forest duty stations or Department of Correction inmates.

Fireline Capability Initial attack
Fireline construction
Firing as directed
Crew Size 18-20
Leadership Qualifications Crew Boss: CRWB
3 Squad Bosses: FFT1
Experience 20% 1 season
